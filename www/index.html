Is ConnectAssist safe to install?

Yes, ConnectAssist uses industry-standard encryption and only allows connections from authorized technicians with the correct credentials. All connections are secure and encrypted.

Do I need to install this every time I need support?

No! This is a one-time installation. Once installed, ConnectAssist runs automatically in the background and your technician can connect whenever you need support.

Will this slow down my computer?

No, ConnectAssist runs quietly in the background using minimal system resources. You won't notice any impact on your computer's performance.

What information can the technician access?

The technician can only see your screen and control your computer when providing support. They cannot access personal files or information without your knowledge and permission.

How do I uninstall ConnectAssist if needed?

You can uninstall ConnectAssist anytime through Windows Settings > Apps, or contact your technician for assistance with removal.
